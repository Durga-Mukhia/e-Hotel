Welcome to e-Hotel Reservation : Browse and Book Hotel of Your Choice!


The e-Hotel Reservation is a web application which lets people browse through the hotels in the country and book hotel(s) of their choice. It is developed in order to make the people's life easier and save time while embracing the beauty of the technology. The application is developed based on Angular framework, JSON-Server and Bootstrap technologies.

One must first register in order to get access to the hotels and hence book them. Users can go to the portal to look for hotels and book any of their choice. Once done with booking, user can go to booking details page to get information about his or her bookings.
